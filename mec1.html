MEC Intermediate Course


MEC full form is Mathematics, Economics and Commerce. It is a popular career field chosen by students completing class 10th to make a career in Finance, Economics, Accountancy, Business and Management, etc. MEC subjects help students have basic knowledge about the field so that they can later opt for related courses for higher education.

Students can master both arts and science stream by choosing the MEC Courses. The best part of choosing the MEC group after matriculation is having a wider range of career opportunities in various streams like the Commerce, Arts, Education, and Teaching field. Students of the MEC group can also see great career scope in Business and Management or in Computer Applications, IT, Finance, Accounting, Marketing
